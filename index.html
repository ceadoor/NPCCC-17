JOBS WITH BUSINESS

Any regular course offered today is basically job oriented, since today’s generation is career minded. A regular income is what everyone needs to lead a secure life. And this wish for a financial security has big role in majority of youngsters not taking up business as a career option. Keeping in mind the choice of today’s generation, 196 NATIONS has come out with an idea which is called “Jobs with Business”.

“Jobs with Business” aims at providing a regular income to those who register with 196 NATIONS and undergo training in business development. Students for this training will be selected on the basis of interview and will be given training for the first 15 days. At the end of training period, those who have the right attitude and skills will be posted as employees by the company in various areas of entrepreneurship. Candidates would be posted anywhere in India, which will assure a better exposure in various fields of business. Apart from the salary, the employee is also assured of a fixed percentage of profit the company earns, where he/she is posted. This definitely would give an added advantage to the candidates as they are entitled to a salaried job and also be part of a business.

196 NATIONS also would give all kind of professional and financial support to all those candidates who are found to have the drive and ability to be an entrepreneur, at the end of the training. Here the candidate can do business, with our support thereby getting a stake in the profit earned by the company.

196 NATIONS aims at building up a society where, through meticulous training people can enter in to the world of business with a positive mind set, leading to numerous opportunities for youngsters in the field of business alone or a job along with a business.
